# Chinese Market Translation Glossary

Preserve these terms exactly:

- Polymarket
- Builder Code
- pUSD
- USDC
- CLOB

Locale guidance:

- `zh-HK`: Hong Kong Traditional Chinese
- `zh-TW`: Taiwan Traditional Chinese
- `zh-CN`: Simplified Chinese

Preferred terms:

- Referral code: `推薦碼` / `推荐码`
- Referral link: `推薦連結` / `推荐链接`
- Invite friends: `邀請朋友` / `邀请朋友`
- Builder fee revenue: `Builder 費用收入` / `Builder 费用收入`
- Pending rewards: `待確認獎勵` / `待确认奖励`
- Payable rewards: `可提取獎勵`, `可提領獎勵`, `可提现奖励`
- Manual approval: `人手審批`, `人工審核`, `人工审核`
- Non-custodial: `非託管`, `非托管`
- User-signed order: `用戶自行簽署訂單`, `使用者自行簽署訂單`, `用户自行签署订单`

Safety meaning must remain intact in every locale: users sign their own orders, the platform does not trade for users, does not custody Polymarket funds, rewards are only based on confirmed Builder-fee revenue, and payouts require manual/admin approval. Avoid income-guarantee and recursive-reward wording.
